LIST OF MOTIVATIONAL SPEAKERS ON YOUTUBE

SANDEEP MAHESHWARI

Sandeep Maheshwari, is an Indian motivational speaker, an entrepreneur and the CEO of ‘ImagesBazaar’ which is the world’s biggest collection of Indian images.

He started off as someone who faced a lot of struggles and disappointments in life and wasn’t clear of his dreams that he wanted to pursue. But after overcoming the obstacles, he realised that his goal in life is to help as many people he can in getting through the hard times. He started working on this dream through “Free Life-Changing Seminars and Sessions”spreading his philosophy ‘To Never Fear of Failures’ and “Be Truthful to self and others”.

https://www.sandeepmaheshwari.com/

DR. VIVEK BINDRA

Vivek Bindra is an Indian motivational speaker, leadership consultant, corporate trainer and a business coach. He is widely known for assisting people in realising their true potential.

Mr.Bindra is associated with more than 1400 corporates,and his biggest achievement is recorded in the Golden Book Of World Record and Limca Book Of World Record for training the largest gathering of HR professionals from across the globe under one roof. His motivation and training methods have helped him achieve numerous awards like that of Best Corporate Trainer by Maruti-Suzuki and Best leadership trainer in Asia award by Marshall Goldsmith.

https://www.badabusiness.com

SONU SHARMA

Sonu Sharma is a famous motivational speaker and the founder of Dynamic Group India. One of the youngest speakers of India, he is known for his successful events in various government and corporate organisations.

In a span of just four years, he has established his global identity where more than a billion people have heard his social media videos in 14 countries across the world. His dynamic and knowledge filled workshops help people in realising their true potential as well as in gaining insights about their business and life.

http://sonusharma.in/

T. S MADAN

T.S. Madan, a winner of the Udyog Rattan Award for his massive contribution in the transformation of the industrial sector, is a motivational speaker and a life coach since the year 1980.

He specialises in imparting knowledge about human behaviour, confidence building and ways to deal with emotions like anger,grief,sorrow,frustration etc. Mr. Madan is not only conferred with a certificate of excellence in the field of management and innovation but he is also a recipient of various other awards from different governmental and non-governmental organisations.

http://www.tsmadaan.in/

HIMEESH MADAN

An international motivational speaker and a performance coach, Himeesh Madan has a specialisation in various fields like that of communication and behavioural skills,team building and leadership values.

His major goal in life is to help his fellow mates achieve success. For supporting this vision, in his initial years he started freelancing in training and worked with various clients including Tata Motors, LIC, Sony,Paytm etc. Through his entertaining, powerful and humorous methods, he has trained more than 100 million individuals till date.

https://himeesh.com/

DR. UJJWAL PATNI

Dr. Ujjwal Patni is an international motivational speaker, author and a famous management consultant. He has successfully trained and inspired several people through his show ‘The Ujjwal Patni Talk show’

he has organised more than 2000 programmes in 150 cities of the world. Dr. Patni’s seven motivational books translated in 12 Indian languages continue to inspire people in their lives. His belief lies in the statement “if you’re not upgrading, you are downgrading.” He is honoured with various awards like The best corporate trainer of India, Top 10 Indian thinkers 2014, Pandit Sundarlal Sharma Rajya Alankaran by Government and many more.

http://ujjwalpatni.com/profile

SNEH DESAI

Sneh Desai is a motivational speaker who helps people in bringing a transformation in their thought process, to ultimately bring a change in their beliefs that hinder their growth,by imparting spiritual knowledge and ways that help in positive thinking.

At a young age of 23, he has influenced numerous lives through his organisation ‘Sneh World’ that is known for conducting different workshops and seminars related to topics like self-development in order to inspire people in realising their true capabilities. He aims to uplift all sections of society irrespective of their age groups, gender or profession.

https://snehdesai.com/

GAUR GOPAL DAS

A former HP engineer, Gaur Gopal Das is currently a famous life coach, monk and a motivational speaker. He is famous amongst youth for the manner in which he explains the subject of spirituality.

In the past few decades, he has conducted several talks nationally and internationally on topics related to life and has guided representatives of famous companies like Infosys, Ford, Bank of America etc. His distinctive methodology of intertwining subtle humour, teachings of the Vedas and the psychology of the younger generation has helped him achieve wonders in the field of self-help industry.

https://gaurgopaldas.com/

SIMERJEET SINGH

With over 13 years of international experience in the motivational speaking space, having served more than 300 organizations worldwide and 80 different nationalities

Simerjeet Singh specializes in customized, engaging & inspirational keynotes and virtual programmes for diverse audiences – from student groups to large multi-national corporations. In 2019, Simerjeet Singh was recognized with the prestigious “Global Excellence Award” (GEA) in Motivational Speaking. He’s also been featured on The Power List of the Top 200 Thought Leaders to follow in 2021 by People Hum and is widely listed among the top 10 Motivational Speakers in India.

https://www.simerjeetsingh.com
