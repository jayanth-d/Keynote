TOP SPEAKERS ON ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND BUSINESS

PAUL ROBINSON

Paul Robinson is one of the most renowned motivational speakers of India. He is known for delivering speeches on essential values like that of leadership, resilience, innovation etc.

His vast knowledge in the field of business comes from the time when he was a serial entrepreneur; not only has he written several audiobooks and ebooks, he is also one of the very few keynote speakers who has successfully delivered speeches in more than 30 global summits. Paul assists people in inculcating qualities that help in personal development and performance improvement. He is also the founder of Positive Revolutions, an organisation that is meant to support other enterprises in overcoming the challenges they face.

https://www.paulrobinson.in

RAJIV TALREJA

Rajiv Talreja is a successful entrepreneur, motivational speaker and a very famous business coach.

Till date, he has influenced and inspired more than 5,00,000 people from across the globe with his stories and sessions. His favourite thing to do after any session is to personally interact with the audience to help them out and understand their issues in a more comprehensive manner. He strongly believes that success is a process that is based on trial and error method and would require you to fall but rise up again with even more courage.

https://rajivtalreja.com/

ANKUR WARIKOO

Born on august 25,1980, Ankur Warikoo is a motivational speaker and an angel investor based in India. He has bagged various awards like LinkedIn India Top Voice 2018, India’s Most Promising Entrepreneur Award (2013) Enterprise Asia March 2014 and many more.

He spends most of his time creating content for educating the masses and guiding instructing students and entrepreneurs through his diverse courses. Through his brand ‘Warikoo Wednesdays’ , he inspires people to do their best and delivers knowledge that he has learned in his experiences through the art of storytelling.

https://ankurwarikoo.com/

SANTOSH NAIR

Santosh Nair is a well-known motivational speaker and a business coach. He is also the founding chairman of ‘smmart training and consultancy services pvt Ltd’

which is one of the most successful entrepreneur coaching and transformation companies of India. Santosh has created hundreds of audio books that focus on awakening human talent and his purpose of “inspiring at least one human life per day.” His intensive training programmes have helped numerous international companies like Vodafone, Thomas cook, Philips, ITW Signode, DLF, Tata Housinggrow and develop in their respective fields.

https://smmart.co.in/

DR. VIVEK BINDRA

Vivek Bindra is an Indian motivational speaker, leadership consultant, corporate trainer and a business coach. He is widely known for assisting people in realising their true potential.

Mr.Bindra is associated with more than 1400 corporates,and his biggest achievement is recorded in the Golden Book Of World Record and Limca Book Of World Record for training the largest gathering of HR professionals from across the globe under one roof. His motivation and training methods have helped him achieve numerous awards like that of Best Corporate Trainer by Maruti-Suzuki and Best leadership trainer in Asia award by Marshall Goldsmith.

https://www.badabusiness.com

MINOCHER PATEL

Minocher Patel is the first and the only motivational speaker from India who was honoured with the ‘KATHA U.K. – Global Excellence Award’ at the House of Lords, London, U.K. for being one of the most influential, humorous motivational speakers known from India till date.

He is also an accomplished national-best selling author who has won several awards like Indira Gandhi Priyadarshini award in the field of training and education. He has trainers numerous national and international people in his career time span of 21 years and continues to work for people by motivating them in fields of leadership, business and motivation.

https://www.minocherpatel.com

SONU SHARMA

Sonu Sharma is a famous motivational speaker and the founder of Dynamic Group India. One of the youngest speakers of India, he is known for his successful events in various government and corporate organisations.

In a span of just four years, he has established his global identity where more than a billion people have heard his social media videos in 14 countries across the world. His dynamic and knowledge filled workshops help people in realising their true potential as well as in gaining insights about their business and life.

http://sonusharma.in/

VARUN AGARWAL

Varun Agarwal is a prominent speaker, an entrepreneur, and an author brought up in Bangalore. He has also delivered INK talk, that is the Indian version of the TedX talks wherein he has shared his life story.

His videos and talks are famous across the world with views of more than ten million. He has lived a fascinating journey of becoming a bestselling author and has opened India’s largest college merchandise company – Alma Mater. It was Varun’s hardwork and resilience that helped the company grow from zero revenue to almost a million in a time span of just three years.

https://varunagarwal.in/

VISHWAS MUDAGAL

Vishwas Mudagal is a motivational speaker, author and a passionate storyteller. His book ‘Losing My Religion’ became the 2014 number one best selling novel that was widely appreciated by audiences of all age groups and professions.

He believes that writing is a way with which he can inspire and influence people and voice his opinions in a much clear manner. Vishwas was also named in the Top 11 personalities of Bangalore in 2014 for his growing popularity amongst the youth. He has been invited to several talk shows and literary festivals where he delivers interactive sessions regarding the art of writing and leading life.

https://www.vishwasmudagal.com
